History and development

We want to move from good to great. Based in Suzhou and founded in 2000, we strive for excellence in urban planning, architecture and interiors, whilst providing creative architectural solutions for clients who look for quality. With seven years of experience planning and building in China, we have realised dozens of high quality projects and know how to turn constrains into potentials.

We start with a five people small studio which works for the local village. At the begining it was hard because none of our architects is famous, we were all graduated students. We have little works during the year, so it is hard to keep our studio running, everyone of us has a part-time job outside the studio. However, we worked hard and try to bring the best product to our costumer, therefore people start to know our studio. We walked step by step, hired more talented architects, and now we are famous and working for the higher level projects. We hope all of our architects and costumers can enjoy the time that working with us.

Chance are always for the people who prepared, if you are interested in our company, come to talk with us.
