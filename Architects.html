Short Description & Design

The building on the left is designed by our famous architect: John Warson, who is graduated from the Harvard University, studied architecture design for more 6 years. He has been hired by Archidea for four years. This year he was in a program which hold by the national government.The building on the left was designed for a open enviornment. It contained with two floors, glass windows and included a 20 meter long pool.

John is a very kind person who love traveling around the world. Our company give our staffs vacation days twice a year, we also covered partial cost of their trip. John went to fifteen countries and applied a lot of foreign elements in his design. The creativity and innovation ability is what we expect to see on our architects. Fortunatly John has both of these two abilities. We are really happy to have him in our company.
